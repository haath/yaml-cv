Profile

This modular generator is used to generate a CV or resume, in HTML or PDF format. The document consist of details parsed from a user-friendly YAML file. This way skills, accomplishments and experienced can be detailed and kept tracked of in a version-controlled manner, without the syntactic challenging of formatting and styling the actual document
